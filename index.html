The Problem

Every chef knows this pain.

You're talented in the kitchen. But calculating food costs, comparing vendor prices, and building event proposals takes hours — time you don't have. One miscalculation and your margin disappears.

  • ⏱
    Hours lost on spreadsheets — manually calculating ounces, calling vendors, doing math by hand for every event.
  • 📉
    Margins eroded by guesswork — underestimating food cost by even 10% on a 150-person event can cost you $500+.
  • 🏪
    No easy vendor comparison — Sysco vs. US Foods vs. Restaurant Depot prices change constantly and nobody tracks them.
  • 📋
    Unprofessional proposals — handwritten quotes and rough estimates lose clients to competitors who look more organized.
